Biography

My name is Izuchukwu Alex Nwachukwu, and I'm a Nigerian currently pursuing a Diploma in Cloud Engineering at AltSchool Africa. My academic journey began with a degree in Agricultural and Applied Economics from Rivers State, which gave me a solid foundation in critical thinking and problem-solving. My keen interest in technology has fueled my passion for cloud engineering, inspired by industry thought leaders and innovators.

Professionally, I have accumulated substantial experience in the humanitarian sector, with a focus on food security, women empowerment, livelihoods, research, and program management. This background has sharpened my skills in strategic planning and problem-solving.

In addition to my professional and academic pursuits, I enjoy reading, cooking, playing football, and hiking, which help me maintain a balanced and active lifestyle.

Why I Joined AltSchool Africa

My decision to join AltSchool Africa's School of Engineering, specifically in the Cloud Engineering program, is driven by my profound interest in technology and my desire to be at the forefront of innovation.

After completing my undergraduate studies, I recognized the immense potential within the tech industry and opportunities it presents for growth and impact.

AltSchool Africa's mission to provide a communal and relatable learning experience aligns perfectly with my aspirations. Being inspired by the success stories of alumni solidified my belief that this is the right place for me. The opportunity to learn from industry experts and collaborate with like-minded peers within a forward-thinking educational community makes AltSchool Africa the ideal environment for achieving my goals and advancing in the tech field.

My Goals for the School of Engineering Programme

  1. Learn a programming language to improve my ability to develop and manage cloud-based applications and services.
  2. Explore and master cloud and DevOps tools to enhance my skills in designing, deploying, and managing scalable and efficient cloud-based systems and applications.
  3. Acquire a deep understanding of cloud platforms and architecture to design and manage scalable, efficient cloud solutions.
  4. Engage with practical, real-world applications of cloud services and DevOps methodologies to enhance my ability to build and optimize cloud infrastructure.
  5. Leverage cutting-edge cloud technologies to contribute to innovative projects and solve complex problems in the digital space.
  6. Collaborate and Network with industry professionals and peers to exchange ideas, foster relationships, and support ongoing learning and career development.
  7. Seek to establish a solid expertise in cloud engineering, positioning myself to take on leadership roles and contribute to cutting-edge developments in the industry.
  8. Aim to emerge as one of the top graduating students in the Karatu 2024 semester, showcasing exceptional academic and practical achievements.
